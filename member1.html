Introduction

Urban traffic has evolved into a complex challenge due to increasing vehicles, limited road infrastructure, and poor routing decisions. Our project models the city as a **dynamic graph**, using algorithms to optimize movement, reduce congestion, and improve safety.

Why Traffic Management?

Modern cities require intelligent systems that automatically adjust signal timings, suggest faster routes, and simulate congestion. Algorithms from DSA help create an efficient, scientific traffic flow model.

Major Traffic Issues

Observed Problems
  • Long queues at peak hours
  • Unbalanced signal timings
  • Frequent merging conflicts
  • Pedestrians crossing without signals
  • Random parking reducing lane width
