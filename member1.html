Smart Traffic Management System

A DAA-based intelligent traffic control system that dynamically manages signals, emergency routing, congestion, and real-time monitoring using graphs, queues, greedy strategies and priority scheduling.

1. System Overview

Traditional traffic systems use fixed signal timings which fail during peak hours, emergencies and accidents. Our Smart Traffic Management System replaces static control with algorithm-driven decision making.

Core Idea:
City roads are represented as graphs, vehicles as queues, emergency vehicles as priority elements, and traffic signals are controlled using greedy optimization.
