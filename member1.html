Smart Traffic Management System

A complete Data Structures & Algorithms (DAA) based urban traffic solution designed to reduce congestion, improve emergency response and enable smart cities.

1. Real-World Traffic Scenario

Modern cities face severe traffic congestion due to population growth, limited road capacity, increasing private vehicles and unpredictable incidents such as accidents. Traditional fixed-time traffic signals are inefficient because they do not adapt to real-time traffic conditions.

Our Smart Traffic Management System solves this by continuously collecting traffic data from sensors and cameras and applying DAA concepts to make intelligent decisions in real time.

  • Traffic signals adapt dynamically based on queue length
  • Emergency vehicles receive priority green corridors
  • Accidents trigger automatic rerouting
  • Public transport is prioritized to reduce pollution
